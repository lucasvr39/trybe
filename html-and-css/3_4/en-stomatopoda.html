Nature's most powerful PUNCH and armor members

The Shrimp Mantis - or the Boxing Lobster , if you prefer nicknames - is the animal with the most powerful punch in nature, whose speed can reach 80km / h . This is required to break their prey shells, but it can also be used to break aquariums. How about creating that pet in captivity? Besides that, its members are so strong that scientists study its cellular structure to contribute to the development of armor for combat troops. Ever thought one Soldier lobster dress? Or is it a shrimp? Well whatever! I wouldn't be surprised if we found some of these little animals fighting MMA in a parallel reality.

More than a dozen Photoreceptors

Most people have about 3 photosreceptors, while Shrimp Mantis has 12 to 16 photosreceptores in its medium tray . Therefore, they are very sensitive in color. These animals can see ultraviolet, being the most complex UV detection system found in nature; which is not necessarily a positive thing. Although they see it as satellites, this makes them worse than other animals when it comes to distinguishing colors. In this case, we can say that less is more, right?
